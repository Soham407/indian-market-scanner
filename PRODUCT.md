# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Solo options trader watching NIFTY during Indian market hours (9:15–15:30 IST). At their desk on a bright morning, making quick entry and exit decisions under time pressure. They open the dashboard before 9:15, keep it visible all session, and rarely scroll — critical data must be readable at a glance. They are not beginners; they do not need explanations, tooltips, or hand-holding.

## Product Purpose

Live monitoring station for NIFTY options. Tracks premium decay (CE/PE price movement from session baseline), Put-Call Ratio from the full options chain, highest open interest strikes (support/resistance levels), yesterday's Nifty OHLC levels, and an AI-generated market commentary ticker. Post-session, it replays historical decay curves for review. Success means the trader sees exactly what matters in under two seconds, with no visual noise.

## Brand Personality

Precise. Trustworthy. Unfussy.

## Anti-references

- Retail broker apps: hand-holdy UI, excessive tooltips, oversimplified charts designed for first-time investors (Zerodha Kite's tutorial overlays, Groww's friendly onboarding feel)
- Crypto exchange aesthetics: neon glow, chaotic data density, dark-purple-on-black color schemes
- Generic SaaS dashboards: pastel card grids, hero-metric templates, light-purple/teal palette

## Design Principles

1. **Signal over decoration.** Color means something. Emerald = CE / bullish signal. Rose = PE / bearish signal. Amber = caution. Slate = neutral data. Nothing is colored for aesthetic reasons alone.
2. **Glanceability first.** The most critical number on any widget must be readable in 200ms. Size, weight, and contrast do this work — not color alone.
3. **Earned density.** Show all the data traders need; hide nothing. But give each piece of data its own breathing room so the eye can land precisely.
4. **No chrome for chrome's sake.** Borders, shadows, and backgrounds exist to organize information, not to look polished. Remove anything decorative that doesn't improve comprehension.
5. **Professional calm.** The interface should not feel tense even when the market is volatile. Steady layout, no blinking, no pulse animations on idle data.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Critical data — PCR value and direction, max-OI strikes, decay positive/negative — must pass WCAG AA contrast at all states. Use both color and shape/label to encode signal direction (never color alone). High-stress glanceability: key numbers at ≥24px, bold weight, sufficient contrast against white.
